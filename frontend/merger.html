How to Use
📋 Step 1 — Export from Readers
Use Rainfall Reader, MinTemp Reader, or MaxTemp Reader to export CSV for each year. Each export = one CSV file.
📂 Step 2 — Upload All at Once
Drag all your year CSVs into the drop zone above. Mix of 1951–2024 = 74 files. Upload all together.
🔀 Step 3 — Merge
Click Merge. Duplicates removed automatically. Sorted by date. One clean combined CSV ready.
📊 Step 4 — Calculate SPI
Download merged CSV and upload it to SPI Calculator. 30+ years needed for reliable WMO-standard SPI.
